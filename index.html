On-going Project

Temporal Pattern Analysis on EDA and POX responses to musical pieces

    This research explores the temporal patterns of psychophysiological responses to musical excerpts by analyzing the expansive Emotion in Motion dataset, the most significant of its kind. Utilizing the Hidden Markov model and Dynamic Time Warping techniques, data from participants is examined across seven countries who listened to three distinct musical pieces. During these listening sessions, Electrodermal Activity (EDA) and Pulse Oximetry (POX) readings were collected, complemented by qualitative feedback from the participants.
